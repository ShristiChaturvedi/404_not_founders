# 404: Not Founders 

> *Turning errors into innovation.*

We are **404: Not Founders**, a passionate team of developers dedicated to building inclusive, AI-powered solutions that simplify access to information and public services. Our mission is to create technology that is practical, accessible, and socially impactful.

---

##  Problem Statement

Millions of people face challenges such as:

- Language barriers in accessing digital services
- Low digital literacy
- Lack of reliable local information
- Rapid spread of fake news and misinformation
- Limited access to government and educational updates

These issues create a digital divide that prevents communities from benefiting fully from modern technology.

---

##  Our Solution

An **AI-powered multilingual voice system** that:

- Understands and responds in multiple regional languages
- Provides location-based local news
- Delivers government and education updates
- Detects and flags fake news
- Works for low-literacy users through voice interaction

---

## ✨ Key Features

-  Voice-based input and output
-  Multilingual support
-  Location-aware news delivery
-  AI-based fake news detection
-  Government & education information access
- ⚡ Real-time updates
-  Secure and privacy-aware design

##  System Architecture

##  Technology Stack

**Frontend:** Flutter / React Native  
**Backend:** Python (FastAPI) / Node.js  
**AI Models:** Whisper, BERT, XLM-R  
**Database:** Firebase / MongoDB  
**Cloud:** AWS / Google Cloud / Azure
